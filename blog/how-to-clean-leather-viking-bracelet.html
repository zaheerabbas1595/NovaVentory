How To Clean A Leather Viking Bracelet

Leather Viking bracelets are easy to wear, but they last longer when moisture, sweat, and storage are handled carefully.

Wipe After Wear

Use a soft dry cloth after wearing the bracelet, especially in warm weather. Removing surface moisture and skin oil keeps the leather from looking tired too quickly.

Avoid Soaking

Do not soak a leather bracelet or leave it wet in a drawer. If it gets damp, pat it dry and let it air dry away from direct heat, which can make leather feel stiff.

Store Separately

Keep leather away from sharp metal jewelry that can mark the surface. A small pouch, tray, or separate section of a drawer is enough for everyday storage.

Remove Before Heavy Use

Take leather bracelets off before swimming, heavy gym sessions, harsh cleaners, or work that creates heavy abrasion. Leather is practical, but it is not meant to be treated like a tool.

Practical Buying Takeaway
